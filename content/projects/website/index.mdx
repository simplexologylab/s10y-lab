This information is to help you understand websites from the ground up to see if there are specific areas that spark an interest. Every day we interact with web sites and apps without even thinking about what is going on behind the scenes. 

```
Honest Disclaimer - If you're just wanting to build a website and you don't care about "how" it actually happens, this information probably isn't for you. There are many great services out like WordPress, Wix, SquareSpace, and many more that can assist with that. 
```

## Learning Areas

All of our projects are intended to spark an interest in various areas of technology and as we dive into the project we'll explore the following with the hope that its simple enough for anyone to pick up. We'll cover:
- Basic elements of web programming
- Web and Graphic Design
- Code management
- Data management
- Site hosting/deployment
- And probably more ...

## Our Goal

You won't walk away from this one as an expert, our hope is that you find something that interest you in this area and we can provide further resources to help assist based on our professional experience in the various fields.
